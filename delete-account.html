Fridge — Account Deletion

You can delete your Fridge account directly in the mobile application.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Fridge app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Open Account
  4. Tap Delete account
  5. Confirm deletion

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently deleted:

  • Your account information
  • Authentication data
  • Your personal fridge data
  • Products and inventory data associated with your account

Shared fridge information

  • If you are a fridge member, your access to the shared fridge is removed
  • If you are the fridge owner, the fridge and related data are also deleted

Data retention

All user-related data is deleted immediately after account deletion.
