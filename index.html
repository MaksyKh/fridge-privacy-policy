Privacy Policy — Fridge

Last updated: April 2026

Fridge ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Fridge mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Fridge app.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Authentication data
  • Account identifiers

Product and Inventory Data

The app allows users to store information such as:

  • Product names
  • Expiration dates
  • Purchase dates
  • Production dates
  • Product quantities
  • Photos of products

Camera and Photo Usage

The Fridge app requests access to the device camera to:

  • Take photos of products
  • Recognize product information using AI features
  • Attach photos to stored products

When AI recognition features are used, photos may be uploaded to secure servers located in Europe for processing and recognition purposes.

Photos are processed only to provide recognition functionality and are not used for advertising purposes.

Device Information

We may collect limited technical information such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Application version

Notifications

The app may send notifications related to:

  • Product expiration reminders
  • Inventory alerts

Notification settings can be enabled or disabled by the user.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide application functionality
  • Manage user accounts
  • Store fridge inventory
  • Process product images using AI recognition
  • Send expiration reminders
  • Improve application performance

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Data Storage

User data and images may be stored on secure servers located in Europe. Reasonable security measures are used to protect data from unauthorized access.

Third-Party Services

The Fridge app may use third-party services required for functionality, including:

  • Cloud hosting services
  • Push notification services
  • AI image processing services
  • Google Play services

Permissions Used

  • Camera — used to capture product images
  • Photos/Storage — used to select images
  • Internet — required for synchronization
  • Notifications — used to remind about expiration dates

Children's Privacy

Fridge is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. If you are under 13 years old, you should use the app with permission from a parent or guardian.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

Subscription Services

The app may offer optional Premium subscription features. All payments are processed through Google Play. We do not store payment information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
